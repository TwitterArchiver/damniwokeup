This is for all of my friends.
Living in such a messed‑up world is not easy. Things never go right, and I’m already tired of them. I do have some mental issues, and sometimes I wonder if it might be better if everything just ended. Yet still, because of you guys, I tread on.

You showed me a spirit that longs for freedom and refuses to ever yield. I’m really into it. Something always held me back or stood in the way, but those extraordinary qualities always reminded me to remain true to myself.

It is my honor to call you my friends. Please keep chasing your dreams, and never forget who you really are. Take care of yourselves and be well. May we all be blessed.

能与你们成为朋友，是我的荣幸。请继续追逐你们的梦想，始终忠于真实的自己。
